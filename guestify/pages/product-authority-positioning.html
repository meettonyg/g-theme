How Does Authority Positioning Work?

The AI analyzes your expertise profile, the specific show's content, and the host's audience to generate positioning assets you can use immediately.

1

Analyze the Show Context

The AI reviews the host's recent episodes, interview style, audience profile, and topical focus. It identifies the specific angles that would resonate most with this host and their listeners, informed by data from Discovery Intelligence.

2

Generate Tailored Expert Angles

Based on the analysis, the AI generates 3–5 expert angles that frame your expertise for this specific audience. Each angle includes a hook, a key insight, and a value proposition that connects your knowledge to what this audience cares about.

3

Build Your Pitch and Talking Points

Select your strongest angle and the AI generates a complete pitch narrative and interview talking points. These assets flow directly into Outreach & Booking so your personalized pitch is ready to send.

Show-Specific Expert Angles

Each angle is crafted for one show and one audience. The AI doesn't generate generic topic suggestions. It analyzes the specific host's content patterns, identifies gaps your expertise fills, and frames your knowledge in the language their audience uses. The result: angles that make hosts say "yes" because the relevance is immediately obvious.

Pitch Narrative Builder

From angle to ready-to-send pitch in seconds. The AI generates a complete pitch narrative: a personalized opening that references the host's work, a clear value proposition for their audience, and 3–4 suggested interview topics. Edit, refine, and send, or use as a starting point for your own voice.

Reusable Positioning Assets

Every positioning asset you create becomes part of your authority library. Expert angles, talking points, and pitch narratives are saved and refined over time. Patterns emerge. You discover which frames resonate most, which angles get the highest response rates, and how your positioning strengthens as your authority builds.
