Podcast Pitch Templates

Five proven podcast pitch templates built on relationship-first principles, not mass-outreach tactics. Each template helps experts start genuine conversations with podcast hosts, demonstrate audience value upfront, and earn interview invitations through relevance rather than volume.

What's Inside the Pitch Templates Pack?

Five distinct templates for different outreach scenarios, each built on the principle that the best pitches start conversations, not transactions. Every template includes the structure, suggested personalization points, and guidance on what makes it work.

  • The Value-First Introduction: For cold outreach to hosts you've never met. Leads with what their audience gains, not what you're promoting. Includes specific personalization prompts tied to recent episodes.
  • The Mutual Connection Warm Intro: For leveraging shared contacts or communities. Frames the introduction around relationship context rather than credentials.
  • The Episode-Specific Pitch: For pitching a follow-up or companion episode to one the host already published. Shows deep engagement with their content and positions your expertise as additive, not competitive.
  • The Return Appearance Request: For hosts you've already appeared on. Frames the ask around new value for their audience and references the relationship you've already built.
  • The Agency Client Pitch: For agencies pitching on behalf of clients. Maintains the relationship-first approach while clearly representing the client's expertise and the agency's role.

Who Are These Templates For?

These pitch templates are designed for anyone who wants to start genuine conversations with podcast hosts. They're the right fit if you are:

  • An expert ready to start outreach who knows which shows to target but isn't sure how to write a pitch that actually gets responses
  • Someone frustrated with low response rates who's been sending pitches that get ignored and wants to understand what hosts actually want to see
  • An agency building pitch workflows that need a proven, relationship-first framework to standardize client outreach
  • A repeat guest looking to deepen relationships with hosts you've already appeared on and want a template for requesting return appearances

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I use these templates word-for-word?

No, and that's by design. These templates provide the structure and principles that make pitches work, but the personalization is what makes hosts respond. Each template includes specific prompts showing you where and how to customize: reference a recent episode, mention a shared connection, or highlight a specific audience insight. The framework is proven; your unique expertise and genuine engagement is what brings it to life.

How are these different from the pitch templates I find on blog posts?

Most online pitch templates are designed for mass outreach: short, generic, and optimized for volume. These templates are built on relationship-first principles. They're designed to start genuine conversations with hosts rather than blast identical messages. The templates lead with audience value (what the host's listeners get), include specific personalization points, and are structured around building trust rather than making asks. Hosts can spot a template pitch instantly; these give you a framework that reads as thoughtful, personal outreach.

Do I need Guestify to use these templates?

No. The templates work standalone for any expert doing podcast outreach. However, Guestify users get additional context that makes every pitch stronger: Discovery Intelligence provides audience data to reference in your pitch, Authority Positioning generates the positioning angles that hosts respond to, and Outreach & Booking tracks every conversation so follow-ups happen on time.
