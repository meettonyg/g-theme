How Does Discovery Intelligence Work?

Discovery Intelligence surfaces the right shows in three steps — from defining your expertise to reviewing a curated, ranked shortlist ready for outreach.

1

Define Your Expertise Profile

Tell the system about your expertise, your target audience, and your authority-building goals. The AI uses this to understand not just what topics you cover, but who you want to reach and why those audiences matter to your business.

2

AI Matches and Ranks Shows

The engine analyzes podcast data across multiple dimensions — topic alignment, audience demographics, host interview style, show credibility, and publishing frequency. Each show receives a strategic fit score that tells you how well it maps to your authority goals.

3

Review Your Curated Shortlist

Receive a ranked shortlist of strategically matched shows with detailed intelligence on each one: audience profile, host background, recent episode topics, and why this show is a strong fit for your expertise. Save shows to your pipeline and move directly into Authority Positioning and Outreach.

Strategic Fit Scoring

Every show is ranked by how well it builds your specific authority goals — not by download count. The scoring model weighs audience alignment with your buyer profile, host credibility and influence within your niche, topic relevance to your expertise, and growth trajectory of the show. You see the score breakdown so you understand exactly why each show made the list.

Audience Intelligence

Know who listens before you pitch, not after. Each recommended show includes audience intelligence: listener demographics, industry composition, and how well the audience overlaps with your ideal buyer profile. You pitch with confidence because you already know the audience is right.

Host Intelligence

Understand who you're pitching before you reach out. Host intelligence includes interview style (conversational, Q&A, deep-dive), typical guest profile, recent episode topics, and engagement patterns. This context feeds directly into Authority Positioning so your pitch resonates with how each host runs their show.

Discovery Intelligence Is Not For Everyone

This tool is built for experts who want strategic, authority-building appearances. It's not the right fit if you're looking for:

  • Mass outreach volume — If your goal is to pitch thousands of shows and hope something sticks, Discovery Intelligence won't help. It's designed to find the 10 right shows, not generate a blast list.
  • Guaranteed placements — The system surfaces strategic opportunities. Earning appearances requires real expertise and genuine relationship building.
  • Biggest shows only — If you only want shows with 100K+ downloads, you're optimizing for vanity. Strategic fit often means mid-tier shows with the exact right audience.
  • Set-it-and-forget-it automation — Discovery Intelligence requires your input to define expertise and goals. The AI augments your judgment, not replaces it.
