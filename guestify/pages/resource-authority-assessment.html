Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Authority Stack?

The Authority Stack is a framework of four compounding layers of recognized trust: Borrowed Authority (trust extended to you by credible voices), Articulated Authority (expertise communicated clearly and consistently), Earned Authority (trust accumulated through consistent value delivery), and Demonstrated Authority (visible results and outcomes your market can verify). Together, these four layers create the kind of authority that makes you the obvious choice in your space. The Interview Authority Assessment measures where you stand across all four.

How long does the assessment take?

About 3 minutes. The assessment includes 10 focused questions that evaluate your current authority across the four layers. There are no trick questions and no "right" answers. The goal is an honest snapshot of where you stand today so you can make informed decisions about where to invest your authority-building effort.

Will I get a sales pitch at the end?

You'll get your personalized Authority Profile with actionable recommendations. Yes, we'll mention how Guestify can help with the specific gaps the assessment identifies, because it's relevant and genuinely useful context. But the assessment results and recommendations stand on their own, whether you use Guestify or not.
