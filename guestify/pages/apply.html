Who Is the 90-Day Partner For?

The 90-Day Authority Launch Partner is designed for experts who want guided support to build Interview Authority on an accelerated timeline. We accept a limited number of partners each quarter to maintain quality. This is the right fit if you:

  • Have genuine expertise but lack the recognition, positioning, or system to translate it into authority that opens doors
  • Are ready to commit 90 days to building a compounding authority asset — not looking for a quick fix or overnight results
  • Want guided support rather than figuring out the system on your own — you value expert guidance and accountability
  • Understand that authority compounds and are willing to treat podcast interviews as relationship investments, not content transactions

This is not the right fit if you're looking for someone to do podcast outreach for you, want guaranteed placements on specific shows, or expect instant ROI from a 90-day investment. Authority is recognized trust, and trust takes time to build.

What happens after the 90 days?

After 90 days, you transition to a self-service Guestify plan with everything you built during the partnership intact: your positioning, your pipeline, your host relationships, and your outcome tracking. There's no lock-in and no dependency on us. The goal of the 90-Day Partner is to build a system that runs independently — you own everything you create.

We limit the program to a small cohort each quarter to maintain the quality of strategic guidance and personalized attention. The exact number varies, but we typically work with fewer than 10 partners at a time. If the current cohort is full, you'll be offered priority placement in the next one. Apply early to secure your spot.
