THE FRAMEWORK

Four Types of Authority. Most Experts Are Missing Half.

Authority Is Recognized Trust. But trust shows up in different ways. You've been doing the work—creating content, networking, hustling—and it still hasn't clicked. Borrowed Authority

Access you don't own (yet)

When a respected host invites you on their show, their credibility transfers to you. That's trust by proximity. Borrowed Authority is access to rooms you couldn't enter alone—and the trust transfer that happens when you're there.

🎬

Articulated Authority

Clarity you do own

Can you explain who you help, what you do, and why you're different—in one sentence that earns trust? Articulated Authority is the clarity that makes hosts want to vouch for you. It's positioning that transfers trust.

⭐

Earned Authority

Being recognized without reintroducing yourself

Earned Authority is when people know who you are before you walk in. It's the reputation that precedes you. It builds through consistent relationships over time—trust that compounds through every interaction.

🏆

Demonstrated Authority

Proof your expertise has market value

Demonstrated Authority is when the market pays you. It's trust validated through transactions—revenue from relationships, deals from partnerships, referrals from hosts who vouch for you to their networks.
