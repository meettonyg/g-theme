How Does the Authority Stack Build Recognized Trust?

The Authority Stack is the four-layer framework that transforms expertise into recognized authority. Each layer compounds on the previous one — and for experts and consultants, the first two layers are where your authority-building journey starts.

1

Borrowed Authority

Access you don't own yet. When a respected podcast host introduces you to their audience, their trust extends to you. Strategic appearances on the right shows build immediate credibility with the audiences that matter most to your practice.

2

Articulated Authority

Clarity you own. Your positioning communicates your expertise before any conversation starts. When hosts, audiences, and referral partners can clearly articulate what you do and why it matters, your authority travels without you being in the room.

3

Earned Authority

Recognized without reintroducing yourself. Sustained, strategic appearances build familiarity. You become the name that comes up naturally when someone asks "Who should I talk to about [your expertise]?" in your market.

4

Demonstrated Authority

Proof your expertise has market value. The relationships you build through strategic interviews become partnerships, referral networks, and revenue channels. Your authority is validated by measurable business outcomes.
