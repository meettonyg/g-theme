AGENCY FAQ

Common Questions From Agencies

How does client data isolation work?

Every client gets a fully isolated workspace within your agency account. Contact databases, outreach pipelines, positioning assets, interview records, and relationship data are completely separated. There's zero data bleed between clients — you'll never accidentally send a pitch from the wrong client's account or mix up contact lists.

Account managers switch between client workspaces instantly from one agency dashboard. Each workspace functions as a complete instance of the Interview Authority System, with its own Discovery Intelligence settings, Authority Positioning profiles, and tracking metrics.

Can clients see their own dashboard?

Agency Operations includes configurable client visibility. You can give clients read-only access to their pipeline and progress metrics, share white-label reports on a schedule, or keep everything internal and present results during review calls. The system adapts to however you manage your client relationships.

White-label reporting removes all Guestify branding, so clients see your agency's brand on every report and dashboard view you share with them.

What does agency pricing look like?

Agency pricing is structured per seat to scale with your client roster. We work with agencies from solo operators managing 3–5 clients to teams managing 20+. Each pricing conversation is tailored to your team size, client volume, and workflow requirements.
