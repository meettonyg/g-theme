Podcast Media Kit Template

A ready-to-customize podcast media kit template that positions you as an authority guest, not just another pitch in a host's inbox. Built for experts who want to look professional, communicate value quickly, and increase their booking rate on strategic shows.

What's Inside the Podcast Media Kit Template?

This template gives you everything a podcast host needs to say yes, organized in a format that communicates credibility and makes their decision easy.

  • Expert Bio Section: A structured format for presenting your expertise, credentials, and unique perspective in a way hosts can quickly scan and evaluate.
  • Topic Menu: A curated list of interview topics with descriptions, audience relevance, and talking points that show hosts you've thought about their listeners, not just your agenda.
  • Past Appearances & Social Proof: A section for showcasing previous interviews, notable media features, and endorsements that build trust before the host even listens to your pitch.
  • Audience Value Proposition: Pre-written framing that helps hosts understand what their audience will gain from your interview, the number one factor in booking decisions.
  • Professional Headshots & Assets: Guidelines for what to include and how to format visual assets that hosts need for show notes, social promotion, and episode listings.
  • Contact & Booking Details: A clean section for scheduling preferences, timezone, technical requirements, and direct contact information.

Frequently Asked Questions

What format is the podcast media kit template in?

The template is provided as a Google Docs file and a Canva template so you can choose the format that works best for your workflow. Both versions include the same structure and guidance. You can also export to PDF once you've customized it, which is the most common format hosts expect to receive.

Do I need to be a Guestify user to use this template?

No. The Podcast Media Kit Template is a standalone resource that any expert can use, whether or not you use Guestify. That said, the template is designed to complement the Interview Authority System. If you use Guestify, the positioning intelligence from Authority Positioning feeds directly into your media kit's topic menu and value proposition sections.

How is this different from generic media kit templates online?

Most media kit templates are designed for influencers or media personalities and focus on follower counts, engagement rates, and brand partnerships. This template is built specifically for experts who want to position their knowledge and credibility, not their social metrics. The structure follows authority-building principles: expertise framing, audience value, and strategic topic selection that hosts respond to.
