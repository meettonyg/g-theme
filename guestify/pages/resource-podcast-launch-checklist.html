Podcast Guesting Launch Checklist

A step-by-step checklist for launching your podcast guesting strategy the right way — from defining your authority goals and building your media kit to landing your first strategic interview. Built for experts who want to start with a system, not random outreach.

What's Inside the Launch Checklist?

The checklist walks you through the complete launch sequence in five phases. Each phase includes specific action items, success criteria, and links to relevant resources so you know exactly what "done" looks like at every stage.

  • Phase 1: Define Your Authority Goals — Clarify what recognized trust means for your specific business, identify your target audience, and set measurable 90-day authority objectives that go beyond vanity metrics.
  • Phase 2: Build Your Positioning Foundation — Create your expert positioning statement, develop your topic menu, and build your podcast media kit so you're ready to pitch with credibility.
  • Phase 3: Research Strategic Shows — Identify 15-20 shows that match your buyer profile, evaluate them for strategic fit (not just size), and prioritize your outreach targets based on authority-building potential.
  • Phase 4: Launch Your Outreach — Use relationship-first pitch templates to start conversations with hosts, manage follow-ups systematically, and convert initial conversations into booked interviews.
  • Phase 5: Prepare and Deliver — Pre-interview preparation checklist, day-of interview best practices, and post-interview follow-up actions that turn one appearance into an ongoing relationship.

Who Is This Checklist For?

This checklist is designed for experts who are starting their podcast guesting journey and want to do it strategically from day one. It's the right fit if you are:

  • New to podcast guesting and want a structured approach instead of figuring it out through trial and error
  • Launching a book, course, or business and want to use podcast interviews as your primary authority-building channel
  • Restarting after failed attempts — you tried podcast guesting before without a system and it didn't produce results
  • An agency onboarding new clients who need a proven launch sequence for client podcast guesting campaigns

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete the entire checklist?

The checklist is designed around a 90-day launch timeline. Phases 1 and 2 (goals and positioning) typically take 1-2 weeks. Phase 3 (research) takes another week. Phases 4 and 5 (outreach and interviews) fill the remaining time. However, you can move faster or slower depending on your availability. The key is completing each phase thoroughly rather than rushing to start outreach before your positioning is solid.
