What Does the ROI Calculator Measure?

Most podcast ROI calculations focus on downloads and impressions — vanity metrics that don't tell you anything about authority or business outcomes. This calculator uses the Interview Authority framework to measure what actually matters.

  • Time Investment Analysis — Calculate the true cost of your podcast guesting effort: research time, preparation, interview duration, follow-up, and relationship maintenance — not just the recording time.
  • Direct Revenue Attribution — Estimate the revenue impact from qualified leads, client conversions, and partnership opportunities that can be directly attributed to podcast appearances.
  • Authority Compounding Value — Model the long-term value of the relationships, reputation, and recognition that podcast interviews build over time — the outcomes that traditional ROI models miss entirely.
  • Strategic vs. Volume Comparison — See the difference in ROI between 10 strategic, authority-building interviews versus 100 random appearances — so you can make informed decisions about where to invest your effort.
  • Personalized ROI Report — Get a summary you can share with stakeholders, partners, or your team that frames podcast guesting ROI in business terms, not media metrics.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the ROI projections?

The calculator provides estimates based on the inputs you provide and benchmarks from experts using the Interview Authority System. Actual ROI depends on many factors: your expertise depth, the quality of shows you target, how well you position your message, and whether you build lasting relationships with hosts. The projections are most accurate for experts who take a strategic, relationship-first approach rather than a volume-based one. Use the results as a planning tool, not a guarantee.

Why does the calculator measure "authority compounding value"?

Traditional ROI models only count direct, immediate returns: leads and revenue in the first 30 days. But Interview Authority works differently — it compounds. A host relationship built today might produce a referral six months from now. An episode published this quarter might be discovered by a decision-maker next year. Authority compounding value models these longer-term, relationship-driven returns that make podcast guesting one of the highest-ROI authority channels available.

Do I need to enter real business data to use the calculator?

You'll get the most useful results with real numbers (average deal size, conversion rate, hourly value of your time), but you can also use estimates. The calculator includes guidance on reasonable benchmarks if you're not sure about specific inputs. Your data stays in your browser — nothing is stored or shared.
