Security at Guestify

Your data is the foundation of your Interview Authority strategy. We protect it with enterprise-grade security so you can focus on building recognized trust, not worrying about data safety.

How Does Guestify Protect Your Data?

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data at rest, in transit, and during processing. Security is built into our architecture, not bolted on as an afterthought.

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use TLS 1.3 for all data in transit and AES-256 encryption for data at rest. Your outreach messages, relationship notes, and business data are protected at every stage.

Access Controls

Role-based access ensures the right people see the right data. Individual accounts have single-user access. Agency accounts support role-based permissions so team members, strategists, and clients each see only what they need.

Data Isolation

Agency client data is fully isolated between workspaces. Multi-client agency accounts maintain strict data boundaries. No client data bleeds into another client's workspace. Cross-client intelligence uses aggregate patterns, never individual client data.

Infrastructure Security

  • Cloud hosting: Guestify runs on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with redundancy across multiple availability zones for high availability and disaster recovery.
  • Regular backups: Automated daily backups with point-in-time recovery capability. Backups are encrypted and stored in geographically separate locations.
  • Monitoring and alerting: 24/7 infrastructure monitoring with automated alerting for anomalous activity, performance issues, and potential security events.
  • Dependency management: Regular updates and vulnerability scanning for all third-party dependencies. Critical security patches are applied within 24 hours of disclosure.
  • Secure development: Code reviews, automated security testing in our CI/CD pipeline, and regular security assessments are part of our development process.

Compliance and Best Practices

  • GDPR compliance: We respect data subject rights including access, correction, deletion, and portability. EU data is handled in accordance with GDPR requirements.
  • CCPA compliance: California residents have additional rights under CCPA. We do not sell personal information and provide mechanisms for data access and deletion requests.
  • Payment security: All payment processing is handled by PCI DSS-compliant providers. We never store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Two-factor authentication: Optional 2FA available for all accounts, recommended for agency administrator accounts.
  • Data export: You can export your data at any time. If you cancel your account, you have 30 days to export before data is scheduled for deletion.

Security Reporting

If you discover a security vulnerability in Guestify, we encourage responsible disclosure. Please report security issues to security@guestify.com. We take all reports seriously and will respond within 48 hours. We ask that you give us reasonable time to address the issue before public disclosure.

Questions?

For security-related questions or concerns, contact us at security@guestify.com. For general privacy inquiries, see our Privacy Policy or email privacy@guestify.com.
